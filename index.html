Mission

A decentralized network that spans the globe and is fueled by a highly performant, open, and user-friendly blockchain combined with a fast and open social blogging network.

Blockchain technology is the driving force in today’s decentralization rush.But we are still facing issues with speed, adoption, and scalability. Dawn solves this by implementing the concept of application-specific blockchains. We introduce a multi-chain system that will combine the best of different technology stacks to create a series of blockchains that are fast, reliable, and open to any other chain on the market.

Technology

Our chains and applications are all written in Go. This makes for a fast, efficient, and clear codebase that lowers development time for blockchain and third-party applications. We will create different chains per application to ensure speed and scalability on a large scale.

Social Media

Dawn mesh is a lightweight but highly customizable social blogging platform written in Go and Javascript meant to stimulate the community and give them an outlet for their ideas. Content creators get rewarded a share of the crypto token when posting popular content.

Torrent Streaming

One of our sidechains will allow the storage of torrent hashes. We’ll combine this with a front-end interface that allows for easy upload and viewing of video content. By using the torrent stream technology, we can create a database of decentralized video content.

The Dawn wallet application

The Dawn wallet is your access to the network and the different tokens and chains the Dawn project will be releasing over the coming months. You can safely store your crypto-assets based on the Dawn concept here. And use our integrated p2p messenger service that is built-in the wallet.

Hardware

By leveraging the best hardware combined with open-source software, we will create a range of open hardware devices that aim to bring affordable quality to users that look for privacy and freedom.

The first device that we will be releasing is a lightweight plug & play validator node for the Dawn network. We aim to release this device in the first quarter of 2017. More devices will be added at a later stage.
